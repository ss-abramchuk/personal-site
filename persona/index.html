Persona

Hi! My name is Sergey, and I’m an iOS developer who’s not afraid to dig deep. I’ve got solid experience in mobile development, a variety of projects behind me, tons of Swift code — and yes, somewhere in the back of my mind, Objective-C is still alive and kicking.

I’ve been lucky to work on truly exciting challenges. Together with an amazing team, we built a video editor Hypee (later was bought by Yandex) where I really got to know AVFoundation, Metal, and CoreImage — a challenge that quickly turned into a joy. Then came BandLab — a music app where I dove headfirst into automation, AI, and giving users creative freedom.

I’m passionate about games — not just playing them, but creating them too. I’ve explored Godot and dabbled a bit in Unity. Game development, to me, is the perfect mix of creativity, math, and engineering.

I’m also into Stoicism — not as a trendy philosophy, but as a practice: accepting what I can’t change and focusing on what I can. It helps me stay grounded not only in life but also in code — calm in the face of deadlines, bugs, and unexpected turns.

I believe technology is just a tool — real magic happens when you put your heart into the work. And that’s exactly what I aim for.
